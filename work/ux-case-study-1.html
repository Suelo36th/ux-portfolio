UX CASE STUDY - Mobile Ordering App for a Sandwich Shop iST

Busy workers and commuters lack the time necessary to prepare a meal.

iST is a regional sandwich shop located in the suburbs of a metropolitan area. IST strives to deliver healthy, specialty sandwiches  and side dishes. They offer a wide spectrum of competitive pricing. IST targets customers like commuters and workers who lack the time or ability to prepare a family dinner.

The Goal

Design an app for iST Sandwiches that allows users to easily order and pick up fresh, healthy dishes.
